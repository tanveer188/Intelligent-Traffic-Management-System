About the Smart Traffic Management System

A robust and intelligent traffic management system leveraging ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) and ATCC (Automatic Traffic Classification and Control) to enhance traffic efficiency and safety. The system integrates real-time monitoring, automated detection, and data analysis for seamless traffic regulation and incident management, security, and improved road safety. By integrating computer vision technologies with real-time data processing, these solutions offer a comprehensive approach to solving traffic-related challenges.
